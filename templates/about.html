About Xender

Founded in 2024, Xender is born from a passion for olfactory excellence. We believe that a scent is more than just a fragrance—it is an invisible signature.

Each bottle of Xender is crafted with the finest ingredients sourced from across the globe. From the deep forests of the East to the floral gardens of the Mediterranean, our master perfumers blend tradition with modern innovation to create timeless masterpieces.

Our Mission

To provide luxury fragrances that empower individuals to express their unique identity through the power of scent.
